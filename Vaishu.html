My Journey

School, college, and everything I learned about people, friendship, and myself along the way. Still being written.

Where I found my talent

I was happy about my talent here — I think I was one of the best students in the class, and I earned a lot of certificates. I always felt I had to prove myself to others, and I was very particular about being on time.

  • Best student in class, several certificates
  • Always punctual
  • Didn't build great friendships — my own behaviour got in the way

Where I found friendship

Everything changed in my next school. I made a lot of friends, tried to be kind to everyone, and finally understood what real friendship meant. The new culture knocked my academics down for a while, but I slowly proved myself again — and enjoyed school more than ever.

"From having no friends to building my own gang — that friendship is still with me today."

They help me, they're happy about my growth, and yes — they still blame me for my mistakes. That's love too.

Walking in nervous

On my first day of college, I thought no one would like me. I was scared I'd make some mistake. Turns out I made a lot of friends — and one especially unforgettable day: my 18th birthday, the best birthday I've ever had. I owe them a sincere thank you.

The setback that taught me to get back up

I had a health issue and my marks dropped. My professor asked me about it — and instead of staying down, I woke myself up, motivated myself, and scored well again. I kept performing well academically. But it still wasn't enough for me.

Lessons in people

Every single semester taught me something new about people and about myself. I wanted to become a topper — and I did. But even that wasn't enough.

5th rank, chasing 1st

By my 6th semester I was ranked 5th in my batch. I want to become the 1st rank holder — and I will. I need to keep shining toward my real goal: becoming a professor.

Missing them already

Now I miss my UG professors and my friends a lot. Thank you to everyone who taught me a lesson, and I'm sorry if I hurt anyone along the way. Thank you, again, to all of them.

A new chapter begins

Now I'm in my PG — and within just one week, I already found the best friends. I hope we always stay together.
